PROJECTS

  • EVENTOMORROW

    During my international internship in Vietnam, I made significant contributions to a project using the Scrum management style. Our collaborative team developed a mobile application for our host company, streamlining event participant tracking, monitoring, and rewards for task completion.

    As a team member, I actively engaged in all aspects of the project, gaining valuable insights into full-stack development. Leveraging Flutter and Firebase, I played a key role in creating an effective and unique mobile application. Flutter enabled me to craft a user-friendly front-end interface, while Firebase provided secure data storage, real-time updates, and seamless communication with the company's servers.

    This experience enhanced my expertise in agile project management, collaboration, and Scrum methodology implementation. It not only expanded my technical skills but also fostered adaptability, teamwork, and meeting client requirements.

  • THE DEN

    I am currently developing a groundbreaking mobile application in partnership with an experienced competitive powerlifter. Our goal is to revolutionize physiotherapy by providing users with a comprehensive repository of meticulously documented information on muscle training, injury prevention, effective stretches, anatomy, and rehabilitation methods.

    I am leveraging my expertise in Flutter and Firebase to create a user-friendly interface and ensure secure data storage, real-time updates, and seamless communication. This project is significant as it addresses the lack of comprehensive physiotherapy apps, empowering users to make informed decisions about their training and rehabilitation. Together, we are shaping the future of physiotherapy knowledge dissemination through innovative technology and a commitment to excellence.
